Payment Terms

We will charge this amount before the conversation starts and after discussing your query we will estimate the time to get solution for you. And depending upon the estimation charges may vary.

Refund Terms

Your amount will be refunded only when you falls under below criteria:

  • You will get your solution in initial 30 minutes of discussion then half of the paid money will be refunded.
  • After discussion for initial 30 minutes if you are not satisfying and you opt not to continue with us, then full amount will be refunded.
  • If any of our assistance is false and you prove then full amount will be refunded.
  • No money will not be refunded if you agree for our solution in initial 30 mins and then later you deny.

Access Terms

We always try to assist you in such a way that you can solve your problem at your self with our inputs but in some cases we may require access to your resources.
